Part 2: Co-Op Game

This game is for two players, it is played on two devices. One of you is a tourist, and the other is a local.
Open the website on both devices and choose a role.

A tourist and a local meet. The tourist wants to get to a place in the city and the local helps by giving directions.

Instructions

• Choose the same map as your partner.
• Choose your role: Local or Tourist.
• Choose the same starting point as your partner.
• Tourist: What place do you want to find? Tell your partner.
• Local: Select the intersections on the map to draw a path to the place that
    the tourist wants to find.
• Local: Give the tourist instructions where to go.
• Tourist: Follow the local's instructions by selecting intersections to visit.
• Finally: Compare your paths and see how you did!
